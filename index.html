PRABAL

Hackathon

"Hack it, till you make it!"

PRABAL is a prestigious national-level hackathon organized under Google Developers Group (GDG) at Sanjay Ghodawat University, Kolhapur. A platform for innovators, developers, and problem-solvers to tackle real-world challenges. Encourages participants to integrate Google technologies into their projects. Focuses on innovation, teamwork, and critical problem-solving under time constraints. Helps participants improve their coding, logical thinking, and project execution skills. Brings together students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts from across the country. Offers mentorship from industry experts, guiding participants through problem-solving. Creates opportunities for networking, collaboration, and career growth. Provides hands-on experience with real-world applications.
